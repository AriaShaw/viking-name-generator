## About
Viking Name Forge is a free online tool that generates authentic Norse warrior names based on historical Viking naming conventions from the Viking Age (793-1066 CE).

## Features
- 80+ authentic Old Norse given names (40 male, 40 female)
- 30+ historical Viking epithets and bynames
- Historically accurate name combinations based on sagas
- Educational content about Viking naming traditions
- Social sharing functionality
- Free to use, no registration required
